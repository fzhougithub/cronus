Introduction
There are two ways to contact the Global Service Desk. 

Instructions
For normal issues please log an Incident or Request via the Service Portal, https://ssctech.service-now.com/ssc.
The Service Portal offers many features such as:
Logging a new Incident or Request
Checking the status of your Incident or Request
Updating your Incident or Request from the Service Portal enables you to communicate with the IT team assigned to your ticket
Leaders can approve ServiceNow Requests
For urgent issues please call the Global Service Desk:
Please note you may experience long hold times.
Use the "call back" feature if you are experiencing a long hold and a tech will call you back same day.
